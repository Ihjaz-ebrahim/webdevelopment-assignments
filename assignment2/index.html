Description

Central Park is bordered by Central Park North at 110th Street; Central Park South at 59th Street;
Central Park West at Eighth Avenue; and Fifth Avenue on the east. The park is adjacent to the neighborhoods
of Harlem to the north, Midtown Manhattan to the south, the Upper West Side to the west, and the Upper
East Side to the east.
